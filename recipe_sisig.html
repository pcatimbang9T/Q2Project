Sisig

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 1/4 lb chicken liver
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 2-3 bird's eye chili peppers, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise (optional)
  • 1 raw egg (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Boil the pork belly in salted water until tender. Allow to cool, then pat dry.
  2. Grill the pork belly and chicken liver until lightly charred. Chop them into small pieces.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped pork and liver to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Season with calamansi juice, salt, pepper, and chili peppers. Mix well.
  6. Optional: Stir in mayonnaise for added creaminess.
  7. Serve hot on a sizzling plate with a raw egg on top. Mix the egg into the hot Sisig before eating.
