Beef Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef stew meat, cubed (chuck or brisket works well)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 potato, sliced into rounds
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup liver spread (or pâté)
  • 1 cup beef broth or water
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup green olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup pickles, sliced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional, for heat)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add beef cubes and brown on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté garlic and onion until softened.
  3. Return the beef to the pot and add the tomato sauce, liver spread, soy sauce, and beef broth. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the carrots, potatoes, and bay leaf. Stir well, then cover and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender.
  5. Once the beef is tender, add the bell pepper, olives, pickles (if using), peas, paprika, and chili flakes. Stir to combine and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If the sauce is too thick, add a little more beef broth or water to reach desired consistency.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
