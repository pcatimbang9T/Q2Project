Bicol Express

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 long green chilies, sliced (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup shrimp paste (alamang), or to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the pork belly and cook until browned and crispy.
  2. Once the pork is crispy, remove excess fat from the pan, leaving a little for sautéing.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan. Sauté until softened and fragrant.
  4. Add the green chilies and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring the mixture to a simmer. Stir to combine.
  6. Add the shrimp paste (alamang), and stir well to incorporate it into the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Allow the sauce to simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  8. Once the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened, remove from heat.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy your Bicol Express!
