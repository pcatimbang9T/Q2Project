Bacon Bagnet

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly (liempo)
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Rub pork belly with salt, pepper, and garlic. Marinate in vinegar for 1 hour.
  2. Boil the pork belly in water until tender. Remove and let cool.
  3. Deep fry the pork belly until golden and crispy.
  4. Slice into strips and serve with a dipping sauce of soy sauce and calamansi.
