Pancit

Ingredients

  • 250g rice noodles
  • 200g chicken or pork, sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in water until soft, then drain.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion in a pan. Add chicken or pork and cook until browned.
  3. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a little water. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the vegetables and cook until tender.
  5. Mix in the noodles and cook until heated through.
  6. Serve hot with calamansi or lime on the side.
