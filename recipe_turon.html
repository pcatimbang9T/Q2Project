Turon

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe saba bananas (or plantains), sliced lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 10 spring roll wrappers (lumpia wrappers)
  • 1/2 cup jackfruit strips (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the bananas lengthwise. If using, cut the jackfruit into strips.
  2. Heat a shallow pan over medium heat and add the brown sugar. Let it melt until it forms a syrup.
  3. Dip each banana slice into the melted sugar to coat it evenly.
  4. Place a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface, and at the center, place a coated banana slice and a few strips of jackfruit (optional).
  5. Fold the sides of the wrapper inward and roll it tightly to enclose the filling.
  6. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Once the oil is hot, carefully fry the turon rolls, turning occasionally until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your sweet, crispy turon!
