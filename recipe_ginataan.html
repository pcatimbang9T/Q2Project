Ginataan

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sticky rice (malagkit), soaked for 1 hour
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 cup jackfruit, sliced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sweet potato, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup saba bananas, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • 1/4 cup tapioca pearls (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the soaked sticky rice, coconut milk, and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  2. Once it starts boiling, lower the heat to simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the mixture thickens.
  3. Add sugar and salt, stirring to dissolve. Taste and adjust the sweetness if necessary.
  4. Next, add the sweet potatoes, bananas, and jackfruit (if using). Continue to cook for another 10-15 minutes, or until the fruits are tender.
  5. If using tapioca pearls, add them at this point and cook until they become translucent.
  6. Once everything is cooked, remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature, and enjoy your delicious Ginataan!
