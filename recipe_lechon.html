Lechon Belly

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 3 stalks lemongrass
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Oil for basting

Instructions

  2. Roll the pork belly and secure with kitchen twine.
