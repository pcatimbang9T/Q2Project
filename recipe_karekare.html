Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 lb oxtail, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 lb tripe, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 lb pork hock, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup rice flour (for thickening)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 banana blossom, sliced (optional)
  • 1/2 bunch banana stalk, cut into pieces (optional)
  • 1 bunch of string beans, cut into 3-inch pieces
  • 1/2 bunch eggplant, sliced
  • 1 tbsp annatto powder (for color)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1-2 cups of water or broth

Instructions

  1. Boil the oxtail, tripe, and pork hock in a large pot until tender (about 2-3 hours). Skim off any impurities from the broth.
  2. Once the meats are tender, remove them from the broth and set aside. Strain the broth and reserve.
  3. In a separate pan, saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  4. Add the peanut butter, rice flour, annatto powder, and fish sauce. Stir to combine.
  5. Gradually add the reserved broth while stirring, making sure the mixture thickens.
  6. Return the cooked meat to the pot along with the banana blossom, banana stalk, string beans, and eggplant. Cook for an additional 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.
